Craigslist Homepage Redesign

When my professor tasked me with redesigning a website utilizing UX best practices I couldn't think of a website more in need of revamp than Craigslist. Craigslist has been a local selling platform since the 1990s, and it appears the interface has not been updated since.
The goal of this project was to address issues found specifically with the landing page of Craigslist. During my research process I found a huge usability issue with Craigslist was its lack of filters and sorting options, its credibility, and its likelihood of scammers.
So my ultimate goal with this redesign was to make an aesthetically pleasing and reliable user interface that would encourage a positive environment of sellers and dissuade scammers.
