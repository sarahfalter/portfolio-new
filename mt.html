Mermaids & Tadpoles

Mermaids and Tadpoles is an up-and-coming swim instruction company located in Jacksonville, Florida. The client was a real-world client brought into my Advanced Web course at the University of Florida.
Thirty students created an entire brand identity and web design for the client's business and had the opportunity to pitch their design at the end of term. Out of thirty students, my brand design was selected by the client to represent her business.

Target Audience: Swimmers ages 3 + in the Jacksonville area and their parents. Goal: Gain more traction and recognition to build a larger client base and trust with parents.
