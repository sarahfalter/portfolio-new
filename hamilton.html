Hamilton Concept Site

This Hamilton concept site is the product of my team of three graduate students at the University of Florida. The goal of this specific project was less about solving a business need and more about learning to code collaboratively using Git and GitHub, as well as incorporating more JavaScript plugins into our designs before diving into vanilla JavaScript.
We decided to design our website around the characters in Hamilton. I displayed them by utilizing a JavaScript carousel combined with a flip card animation to show the Broadway character and the real-life character on the other side.
